Jeddah blends rich history with a vibrant coastal lifestyle. Stroll along the Corniche, explore historic Al-Balad, and enjoy world-class dining and art by the Red Sea.

About Jeddah

Jeddah, Saudi Arabia's gateway to the Red Sea, is famed for its historic Al-Balad district, vibrant souks, and modern waterfront. The city is a melting pot of cultures, offering a unique blend of tradition and cosmopolitan flair.
